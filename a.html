Our Story

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2015 by Chef Elena Marchetti, Aurelia was born from a vision to create more than just a restaurant — it was designed to be a destination where every element conspires to create an unforgettable experience.

Our philosophy centers on honoring traditional techniques while embracing modern innovation. Each plate tells a story of heritage, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to quality that has earned us recognition among the world's finest dining establishments.
