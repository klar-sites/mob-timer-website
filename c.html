Our Story

Rooted in craft,
driven by curiosity

Maison Aurèle was born from Chef Laurent Marchand's obsession with fire-driven cooking and the farmers who shape our terroir. Every plate tells the story of a particular morning at the market, a conversation with a winemaker, or a childhood memory from Périgord.

We do not chase trends. We chase honesty — in sourcing, in technique, and in the hospitality we extend to every guest who walks through our doors.
