About the Clinic

A tradition of surgical precision

Ashworth Clinic was founded on a single principle: that exceptional surgical outcomes demand both technical mastery and genuine attentiveness. Our consultants hold fellowships from leading international centres and bring a combined expertise spanning reconstructive shoulder surgery, complex hand trauma, and microsurgical repair.

Every treatment plan is built around the individual — their anatomy, their goals, their daily demands. We do not believe in one-size-fits-all protocols. From the first consultation to final rehabilitation, you are guided by the same surgeon who operates.

Our Specialties

Focused expertise in upper limb surgery

Rotator Cuff Repair

Arthroscopic and open repair techniques for partial and full-thickness tears, restoring range of motion and strength.

Shoulder Instability

Stabilisation procedures including Bankart repair and Latarjet for recurrent dislocations and subluxation.

Carpal Tunnel Release

Minimally invasive decompression surgery for median nerve entrapment, with rapid return to daily function.

Hand & Wrist Fractures

Precise fixation of scaphoid, metacarpal, and distal radius fractures using advanced plating and screw techniques.

Sports Injuries

Tailored surgical and rehabilitative pathways for athletes — from ligament reconstruction to tendon repair.

Tendon Reconstruction

Complex flexor and extensor tendon repairs and staged grafting, restoring grip and dexterity.
