Brand Identity + Web

Aster Studio Identity

Aster Studio needed a more elevated presence to attract premium residential clients. The solution combined a restrained monochrome identity, a memorable accent color, and a portfolio-led website that framed projects as stories rather than static galleries.

+38% Qualified inquiries
+24% Time on site
+31% Portfolio clicks
  • Developed a cleaner narrative structure across homepage, portfolio, and services.
  • Created a scalable visual system for proposals, social media, and presentations.
  • Improved lead quality by clarifying positioning and ideal project scope.
Product UX + Interface Design

Northstar Finance App

Northstar’s product had powerful features but a confusing interface. I redesigned the user flow to simplify onboarding, reduce visual noise, and introduce clearer hierarchy in the dashboard experience.

-22% Drop-off in onboarding
+19% Feature adoption
4.8/5 User testing score
  • Mapped high-friction user paths and simplified navigation patterns.
  • Built reusable UI modules for consistency across product screens.
  • Introduced cleaner data presentation using spacing, contrast, and progressive disclosure.

About Me

Designing with clarity, personality, and purpose

I’m Elena, a freelance graphic and UI designer working with founders, startups, and creative brands to shape identities and digital experiences that feel both refined and effective. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, storytelling, and visual precision.

I believe great portfolios and brand experiences do more than look beautiful — they create context, build trust, and help people understand why your work matters. That’s why every project starts with the narrative behind the design, not just the layout itself.

Approach Research-led, collaborative, and built around strong hierarchy.
Tools Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Webflow, Framer, and design systems.
Best fit clients Founders, lifestyle brands, SaaS teams, and creative studios.
Location Available remotely for clients worldwide.
