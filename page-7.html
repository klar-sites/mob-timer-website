Our Story

A Passion for Perfect Coffee

📷

Café Interior

6+ Years of Excellence

Founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiasts Maria and James Chen, The Velvet Bean began as a small dream to bring specialty coffee to our neighborhood. What started as a single espresso machine and a handful of regulars has blossomed into a beloved community gathering place.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Each batch is carefully roasted in-house to bring out unique flavor profiles that tell the story of their origin.
