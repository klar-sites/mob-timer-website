Our Story

A Legacy of Passion

Nestled in the heart of the city, Aurea was born from a singular vision: to create a dining experience where every plate tells a story and every evening becomes a memory.

Our Executive Chef, Isabelle Moreau, draws inspiration from the markets of Provence, the coastlines of Liguria, and the vibrant terroir of Northern California. Each dish celebrates the raw beauty of seasonal ingredients, transformed through classical technique and modern imagination.

We believe that dining is an art form — one that engages all the senses. From our hand-thrown ceramic tableware to our curated wine cellar, every detail is considered.

— Chef Isabelle Moreau
