Our Story

A Legacy of Culinary Passion

Founded in 1998 by Chef Antoine Beaumont, Aurelian has become a beacon of gastronomic excellence in the heart of the city. Our philosophy is simple — source the finest ingredients, honor traditional techniques, and create unforgettable moments at every table.

Every dish tells a story of heritage and innovation, crafted with precision by our award-winning culinary team. From the first amuse-bouche to the final petit four, dining at Aurelian is a journey through flavor, texture, and artistry.
